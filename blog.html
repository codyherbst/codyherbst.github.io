Was your first week what you expected? Why? Why not?

For the most part the first week is what I expected. Going through relatively basic concepts on github seems to be a natural first step towards the end goal of being a developer. However experiencing as much of a struggle o learn these concepts is a refreshing experience that I have not had for some time.

What are you struggling with?

The main struggle was understanding how to get this blog post to work as I was a bit lost in the beginning as far as where to put this text and how to set up folders on github using git. Other than this command line has been a bit of a struggle to learn properly. That or maybe it is just experiencing the learning curve.

What are you excited or eager to learn more about?

I'm very eager to begin coding and becoming familiar with our future work. This is the same feeling I have as when you start a new job and have that lost feeling trying to learn the area. It's a daunting feeling but I cannot wait to overcome it since it is such a satisfying feeling to do.
